Dallas Fort Worth International Airport

DFW Dynamic Model — 4D Construction Viewer

I built the DFW Dynamic Model to give construction and operations teams a single place to see what's happening across the entire airport campus in 4D. It connects live BIM models to a schedule timeline so you can scrub from 2026 to 2031 and watch the airport transform.

  • Interactive timeline scrubber spanning 2026 to 2031, covering all terminals and airfield zones
  • Color-coded project overlays showing active closures for runways, taxiways and gates in real time
  • Layered legend with toggles for Airfield, FAA Requirements, Airlines, Delays and Facilities
  • Multi-model BIM loading through Autodesk APS and ACC using SVF2 format and two-legged OAuth2
  • Node.js backend handling S3 uploads with signed URLs and translation status polling per model

NYC Department of Transportation

Infrastructure Asset Management & Inspection Platform

Built for NYCDOT to manage thousands of city infrastructure assets across all five boroughs. Inspectors log field data on their phones, managers see dashboards, and the system generates printable PDF reports from live records.

  • Separate role-based views for field inspectors, managers and executives
  • Search and filter across asset type, condition rating and borough with instant results
  • Condition trend charts showing inspection history over time for each individual asset
  • One-click printable PDF reports generated from live database records

NYC Department of Buildings

Multi-Stage Document Review & Comment Workflow

NYC DOB needed a way to replace their paper-based permit review process. I built a structured workflow where each application moves through four stages, with role-gated approvals and a full comment thread at every step.

  • Four-stage pipeline from Submission through Technical, Legal, and Final Approval
  • Role-based access so applicants, reviewers, supervisors and admins each see only what they need
  • Threaded comments with open and resolved states, plus email notifications on status changes
  • Full timestamped audit log of every status change and comment across each application
